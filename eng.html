About us

We are a dynamically developing family company with many years of experience. We have been operating on the market since 1994. Our headquarters is located in Kluczbork.

Thanks to an experienced and qualified team, their commitment and effective organization of work, we can flexibly approach all the needs of our customers by implementing large orders at the highest level.

Orders are carried out strictly according to the customer's instructions so as to achieve 100% satisfaction.

The goal of our company is the production of all types of covers on behalf of external customers. We are currently implementing projects for contractors from Western European countries such as Germany, the Netherlands and Austria, and we are constantly cooperating with long-term partners from Poland.

Cutting service

We also have our own cutting room. We offer cutting service with a Zünd G3 XL cutter.
Thanks to modern technology, we are able to create optimal configurations from templates which provides a significant reduction of waste.
We can guarantee the highest efficiency and quality of the cut elements. Maximum fabric width: 2,2 meters.
